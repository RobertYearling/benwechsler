What I Am Doing Now

Updated Monday, June 12, 2017

I recently uprooted myself and moved from Chicago to Durham, NC, and have found this to be the most positive change I ever made for myself. The climate, people, and professional talent in the Raleigh-Durham metropolitan area, including the Research Triangle Park are highly exceptional, vibrant, and open.

Currently, I am focussing on these projects:

  • Building my leadership development & strategy consultancy.
  • Working out daily and maintaining a healthy diet.
  • Creating a Branding of Your Leadership course and presentation.
  • Teaching technology and leading people to be empowered by technology rather than being dictated by technology.

When I am not doing something listed above, I am usually solving a puzzle, listening to music, reading a mystery. I prefer mysteries with strong detectives like Nero Wolfe, Ellery Queen, Hercule Poirot, and am fond of Special Agent A. X. L. Pendergast.

I love the Chicago Symphony Orchestra and will stop dead in my tracks to hear them in person.

My fervent belief is that every human being on earth is unique and has unique knowledge. I love to lead people to discovering their unique knowledge and to help them broadcast their uniqueness.

This page is inspired by Derek Sivers and the Now movement at http://nownownow.com.
